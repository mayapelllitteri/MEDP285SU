History of Plum

Origins

The color plum was first recognized during acient times when plum fruit skins were used to make dye and color garments. These dyes were most used for clothing worn by royals as a representation of their high social status. As a result, plum the color became associated with luxury and monarch.

Composition

Plum can be descriped as a dark purple with brown and redish tones. More specifically, it is an equal combination of the tertiary colors slate and russet. Plum can come in the a variety of shades from dark to light. Hex number: #8E4585

Meaning and Associations

In modern times, plum is symbolic of creativity, mystery, comfort, and imagination. The rich nature of the color makes it elegent and bold, yet cozy. Plum (hex #8E4585) a depth that seems almost swimable.
